Our Story

We Are
MindNest Games

MindNest Games is an independent mobile game studio based in the heart of Lahore, Pakistan. We started with a simple belief — that great games don't need massive budgets, just big ideas and genuine passion.

Our small but dedicated team crafts fun, polished, and accessible mobile games for Android players around the world. From brain-twisting puzzles to heart-pounding racing and epic RPG adventures, every title we ship reflects our obsession with quality and delight.

We're proud to be building something from Pakistan that reaches players globally — and we're just getting started.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your data.

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

MindNest Games ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information in connection with your use of our mobile games and this website. By using our products, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Game progress, scores, session duration, and in-game interactions.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting and performance optimization.
  • Advertising Data: If you view ads within our games, third-party ad networks may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we may retain your name and email address to respond to you.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, improve, and personalize our games.
  • To analyze crashes and performance issues.
  • To display relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks.
  • To respond to your support inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

Our games may integrate third-party services including Google Play Services, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Unity Ads. These services have their own privacy policies and data practices that we encourage you to review. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Some of our games may be suitable for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@mindnestgames.site and we will promptly delete it.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, please email us at support@mindnestgames.site.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page regularly. Continued use of our games constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us:
